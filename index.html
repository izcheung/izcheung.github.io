ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Irene! I transitioned from working in the science and healthcare industry into the tech industry after finding a newfound passion for programming. Currently, I am a student in the Computer Systems Technology program at BCIT, where I got into its competitive Co-op program and gained hands-on experience at a fast-paced startup. During my internship, I got experience programming in modern technologies such as TypeScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, and MongoDB, earning a promotion within five months due to my adaptability and drive to learn.

I am excited to apply my skills in software development, web development, and UX/UI design in a full-time role starting January 2026. My strong problem-solving and analytical skills, driven by my unique scientific background and eagerness to learn, help me tackle tech challenges effectively.

Outside of programming, I enjoy traveling, hip-hop dancing, photography, and digital art. I am always eager to learn and collaborate on meaningful projects.
